Skills for Job creation.

The Ministry of Skills Acquisition and Entrepreneurship Development is tasked with the crucial responsibility of designing, initiating, and implementing various skills acquisition programs aimed at creating employment opportunities and reducing poverty across the state. These programs are carefully structured to equip individuals with practical knowledge and technical expertise in diverse fields, enabling them to become self-reliant and contribute effectively to the economy. Beyond providing vocational training, the ministry also focuses on fostering entrepreneurship, encouraging residents to start their own businesses and engage in productive ventures that generate income and promote economic growth. By promoting innovation and enterprise development, the ministry seeks to nurture a culture of creativity and resilience among citizens. The overarching goal is not only to address immediate unemployment challenges but also to empower individuals with sustainable skills that enhance their long-term employability. Through these initiatives, the ministry plays a pivotal role in the social and economic transformation of the state.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide innovative solutions to address the gaps in skills acquisition and entrepreneurship development while promoting sustainable development and social responsibility

Our Vision

Our vision is a Sokoto State where every youth is empowered with opportunities, skills, and purpose, eliminating redundancy, preventing restiveness, and fostering sustainable progress.
